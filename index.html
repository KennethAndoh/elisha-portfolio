About Me

Elisha Akanlo Anankansa is a Geomatic Engineer with specialized expertise in Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Systems (GIS). He earned his B.Sc. (Hons) from the Department of Geomatic Engineering at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Ghana. Elisha has worked with the Environment Protection Agency of Ghana on projects focused on urban flood resilience. During the 2022/2023 academic year, he served as a Teaching and Research Assistant at KNUST, contributing to both academia and research. With a career objective aimed at addressing sustainability challenges, his interests span climate change, GIS, and ecological systems. Educational background:BSc Geomatic Engineering, Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Ghana
